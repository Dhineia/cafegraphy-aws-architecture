About Us

Coffee + Photography
A perfect place to hang out with your friends while everyone , can enjoy a sip while relaxing one's senses w/ shutters and shots of life.

The owner's of Cafegraphy are Kyro Loanzon and Jerald Rosal, they are both 19 and 25 years old when they started their business and build Cafegraphy. The theme of the cafe is Japanese and it is Kyro who came up the idea. It is because when Kyro went to Japan, he was inspired of the good culture and dashing restaurants with a kotatsu. The origin of Cafegraphy comes from when Kyro thought that everybody loves photography and people stay and always go to coffee shops. Combining the two ideas, they build a good and unique brand.
